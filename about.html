About Real Estate With Tofunmi

Your Trusted Partner In Property Wealth Creation

Who We Are

Welcome to Real Estate With Tofunmi, where your property goals meet the power of informed investing.


We are a dual-purpose real estate brand dedicated to helping individuals, families, and investors secure premium properties while gaining the knowledge required to make smart real estate decisions.


We believe real estate is more than buying land or buildings. It is about creating financial security, preserving wealth, and building a legacy that lasts for generations.

What We Do

Premium Real Estate Sales

We source, verify and connect clients with high-potential real estate opportunities.


From residential properties and commercial assets to land banking, land flipping opportunities and agricultural investments, our goal is to help clients acquire valuable assets with confidence.

Practical Real Estate Education

Knowledge protects investments.


Through educational content, market insights, and investment guidance, we help individuals understand property acquisition, appreciation, ROI strategies, documentation and market trends.
